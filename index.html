Founder & Principal Advisor

Maria Filippova Barnachinskaya is an international business development executive and strategic advisor with experience spanning the GCC, Europe, North America and emerging markets.

Over the past decade she has facilitated cross-border partnerships, supported international expansion initiatives and worked alongside investors, family offices, professional service firms and multinational organizations.

Fluent in English, Russian and Spanish, Maria combines commercial insight, cultural intelligence and an extensive international network to help ambitious organizations accelerate growth and access new opportunities.
